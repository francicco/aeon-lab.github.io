Ancient Rapid Radiation Underlies Persistent Phylogenomic Conflict in Early Collembola Diversification

A 145-taxon, 1,127-USCO phylogenomic study of springtails resolving why early collembolan relationships have remained unstable — the conflict itself turns out to be the signal of a genuine ancient rapid radiation.
